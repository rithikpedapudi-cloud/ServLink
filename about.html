About ServLink

ServLink was founded to solve the Local Services Trust Problem. We recognized that traditional service marketplaces suffered from two major issues: high commission fees that hurt local providers, and a lack of authentic community trust that left customers uncertain.

Our Core Mission

  • • Build Trust: Every provider earns a Trust Badge through verified community endorsements, not just transactional ratings.
  • • Empower Providers: Our low-fee model (or subscription for 0% fees) ensures providers keep more of their hard-earned income.
  • • Strengthen Community: We facilitate direct, transparent connections, turning transactions into valuable local relationships.

Join the Movement.

Whether you're looking for a reliable neighbor to fix a leak or seeking to grow your local service business fairly, ServLink is your transparent, community-first platform.
