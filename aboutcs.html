Our Mission: Trust, Not Transactions

ServLink was born out of frustration with the status quo. We're here to solve the **Local Services Trust Problem** by cutting out high-fee intermediaries and strengthening genuine community connections between customers and verified providers.

Verified Trust

Trust is earned locally. Our **Trust Badge** is gained through community endorsements, not just simple ratings, ensuring you get reliable, vetted service.

Financial Fairness

Providers keep their earnings. Our **low-fee or 0% commission subscription model** eliminates the 15-30% cuts typical platforms demand, supporting local business growth.

Direct Connection

We facilitate direct interaction via our Chatbot and booking system, streamlining requirements and fostering long-term relationships, not just one-off sales.
